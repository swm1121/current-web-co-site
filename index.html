FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a project take?

Most projects launch within 2–4 weeks after all required content and information are received. Timeline depends on how quickly we receive materials and feedback.

What if I don't have my website copy written?

That's fine — most clients don't. Send us your notes, menus, brochures, social media content, or any existing materials. We'll organize and write professional copy for your site. You review everything during the revision round before it goes live.

How does payment work?

50% deposit to begin the project. The remaining 50% is due before the site launches. No surprises, no additional fees outside the agreed scope.

What if I need more than 5 pages?

Additional pages are $250 each, added to the flat-rate build before the project starts.

What does the revision round include?

After the first complete draft, you review the full site and submit changes. We make those updates before launch. Additional revision rounds are available at $250 each if needed.

Do I own my website?

Yes. Your domain, content, and website files belong to you. The Website Care Plan is optional — you're free to move your site to another host at any time.

Do I need to already have a domain?

No. If you already own one, we'll connect it. If not, we'll walk you through getting one.

What's included in the Website Care Plan?

Hosting, uptime monitoring, security updates, DNS management, backups, up to one hour of minor content updates per month, and email support. Your first year is included with every build. After that, we send an annual invoice at $695/year — service continues once it's paid. No automatic billing. After year one, you can renew or move your website to any host you choose — everything you paid for stays yours.

What if my project isn't a good fit?

We'll tell you before taking a deposit. We only take projects we're confident we can deliver well.

Do you offer ecommerce or online stores?

No. Current Web Co is built for professional informational websites — not storefronts or custom web applications.

Why is your pricing lower than most agencies?

Most small businesses don't need a $10,000 custom website or a large agency team. Current Web Co focuses on straightforward websites with a defined scope, fixed pricing, and a simple process. Lower overhead means lower prices — without cutting corners on the work.

Do I need to be located in the Northern Mariana Islands?

No. Current Web Co works remotely with businesses in the CNMI, the United States, and internationally.
