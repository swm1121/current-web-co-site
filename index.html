FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a project take?

Most projects launch within 2–4 weeks of approval, depending on how quickly we receive your content and feedback.

What if I need more than 5 pages?

Additional pages are $250 each, added to your flat-rate build.

What happens after the first revision round?

Additional revision rounds are available for $250 each. Most projects are resolved within the included round.

Do I need to already have a domain?

No. If you already own one, we'll connect it. If not, we'll walk you through getting one.

What's included in the Website Care Plan?

Hosting, uptime monitoring, and security updates. Your first year is included with every build. After that, we send an annual invoice at $995/year — service continues once it's paid. No automatic billing.

Can I make changes to my site after launch?

Minor content updates can be handled through the Care Plan. Larger changes are scoped as a Website Refresh, starting at $995.

Do you offer ecommerce or online stores?

No. Current Web Co is built for businesses that need a professional informational website — not a storefront. See "Who We're Not For" above.

What if my project isn't a good fit?

We'll tell you directly during the review step, before any money changes hands.
